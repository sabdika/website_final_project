ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Admission Procedure

Eligibility

Students who have passed SEE examinattions with minimum B in aggregate are eligible to apply for science stream and with minimum C+ grade are eligible to apply for management and humanities.They are required to appear into written entrance examination followed by interview.Only those who get through entrance test wil be eligible for enrollment.

How to apply

Students can obtain appllication form from the reception desk at college.The applicants can also download the application form from the college website. and submit it to the collge upon payment.

Things to submit

  • Two passport sized colour photographs
  • Copies of SEE Grade and Character Certificate
  • A reciept of application processing fee set by college
